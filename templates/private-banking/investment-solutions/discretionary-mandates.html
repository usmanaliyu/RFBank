Discretionary Mandates

Because time is your most valuable asset

Private Banking with a relentless focus on your goals

Time is your most valuable asset, which is why you would like to delegate investment decisions to an experienced investment professional. You need a solid and reliable partner who can manage your portfolio of assets to grow and protect your wealth – with a relentless focus on your interests and those of future generations. A trusted partner, for the long term.

At EFG, our expertise in portfolio management, combined with the most advanced asset management techniques, means that you can be confident your assets are in the hands of trusted professionals, allowing you to dedicate more time to the things that matter most to you.

You can delegate the management of your wealth through our innovative, individually tailored discretionary mandates. They are designed to generate performance according to your investment objectives and risk profile. In this way, you can be assured that your investments are being carefully monitored at all times while benefiting from our first class investment expertise.
