EFG access

Your quick and secure validation process

Prioritising speed and security

The ability to access and move money is important to you. But that flexibility should never come at the cost of security. Which is why EFG Access is a vital step in our process of protecting your wealth. And why you can be confident that keeping your money safe is our priority, while you take control.

Empowering fast executions

EFG Access allows you to quickly and securely validate payments from your eBanking. You can enter payments in your eBanking accounts as usual and then validate them for execution using the EFG Access app. The validation and authentication are thereby in line with state-of-the-art security requirements and allow you to securely validate external payments via the app or using double signature authentication, if required. EFG Access is aimed at EFG clients with European accounts. The application is in line with the European Payment Service Directive (PSD2), meeting the strong client authentication requirements that take effective as of 14 September 2019.
