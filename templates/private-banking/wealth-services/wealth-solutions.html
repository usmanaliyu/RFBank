EFG Wealth Solutions: Putting your hard work to work

We specialise in the efficient structuring, protection and transferral of wealth.
Our teams of experienced professionals operate in various locations, including Jersey, Luxembourg, the Bahamas and Singapore. Working alongside you, your Client Relationship Officer and any tax or legal professionals, we craft bespoke solutions to protect your assets and facilitate the efficient transfer of wealth between generations.

Our people

Our wealth structuring specialists are highly experienced, technically proficient professionals. They also have strong relationship skills and always seek to be pragmatic and solution-driven. They operate from various locations, including Jersey, Luxembourg, the Bahamas and Singapore. While used to dealing with complex, cross-border considerations, they strive to keep things simple for you.
