Administration and support solutions for investment managers

EFG Funds Services is a division of EFG Wealth Solutions.

EFG Fund Services provides administration, custody and exchange listing services to a wide range of closed-ended and open-ended funds.

Our main offices are in Jersey and Luxembourg, and we also have a presence in Cyprus, Cayman Islands and Singapore. This means we can be truly jurisdiction-agnostic in devising solutions for our clients.

Our aim is to deliver a high level of client service to both the fund and its investors, underpinned by technology and a pragmatic, proactive approach.

What we offer

At EFG Fund Services, we recognise the individuality of each fund and our teams work closely with each client to build a detailed understanding of the needs of their fund and investors.

We pride ourselves in providing a client focused service offering solutions to cater for their needs.

  • In-house Banking Solution: Our in-house banking and payments ensure faster turnaround times, joined up processes and ability to unlock challenges and issues easily.
  • Bespoke Service: We aim to provide a flexible and tailored service to our clients to fit their needs
  • Experienced and Stable Team: Our Directors have extensive board and Fund Administration experience and are supported by our team of experienced colleagues and team members
  • World Class Technology: We use a leading fund administration platform, allowing high rates of Straight Through Processing (STP) and great client service.
