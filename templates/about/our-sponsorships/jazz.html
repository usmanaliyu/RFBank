Our jazz partnerships

Explore our long standing, harmonious partnerships

#wearejazz

Jazz is perhaps the most entrepreneurial of musical forms. The perfect harmony of talent, skill, creativity and collaboration. A genre inhabited by musical entrepreneurs who think differently. And through their innate talent, create the truly exceptional.

We are proud to have been part of the global jazz family for over a decade. We celebrate and aspire to share the distinctive qualities that make it exceptional, embracing creativity and innovation, diversity and collaboration.

EFG London Jazz Festival

We have been a committed supporter of the EFG London Jazz Festival since 2008, and headline sponsor since 2013.

The UK’s annual celebration of jazz delivers live performances by an electrifying line up of global stars and emerging artists, special collaborations and the finest and freshest new music.

Discover more about the EFG London Jazz Festival

Jeremy Monteiro

We've supported jazz across Asia since 2008 and are a partner of renowned Singapore based jazz pianist and composer, Jeremy Monteiro. Known to many as Singapore’s ‘King of Swing’, Jeremy is an EFG ambassador and regularly plays on the international jazz circuit.

Jazz Roots, Miami

EFG is the Presenting Sponsor of Jazz Roots, a six part concert series held at the Adrienne Arsht Center for the Performing Arts of Miami-Dade County. Jazz Roots celebrates artistic diversity and creativity and cultivates young and emerging talent from Miami and beyond. Previous Jazz Roots Series have featured some outstanding musicians, including Sergio Mendes, BB King, Patti Austin, Gregory Porter, Wynton Marsalis and Cécile McLorin Salvant.
