EFG London Jazz Festival

Bringing people together to create something special

Playing solo? A perfect partnership performs better

We’re exceptionally proud to be the committed headline sponsor of the EFG London Jazz Festival. For almost 15 years we’ve been powering this globally renowned exhibition of the best in the business, from our headline event, Jazz Voice to our EFG Elements series. The EFG London Jazz Festival turns 30 in 2022 and it promises to be a year of special celebrations.

Welcome to the 30th EFG London Jazz Festival: 11 to 20 November 2022
