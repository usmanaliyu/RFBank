Southbank Sinfonia

Discover the unique orchestra that provides a springboard for intrepid young talents

In perfect harmony

Each year, Southbank Sinfonia welcomes 33 of the worldâ€™s most promising graduate musicians to embark on its renowned fellowship. More than just an orchestra, this is a community where talented young musicians can find their own creative strengths, take their musicianship to new frontiers and have a uniquely important voice in the sector, challenging and refreshing traditions.

Bringing back the music with entrepreneurial thinking

In 2020, live music fell silent. So in October, EFG collaborated closely with our partners Southbank Sinfonia and Historic Royal Palaces to create a unique recording of specially selected music, filmed at the Tower of London. The result is an evening of uplifting music from these inspirational young musicians.

The Fellowship

33 exceptional talents bringing their unique skills and personality to the music to form an orchestra like no other.  Find out about this year's young musicians.

Explore

From new beginnings to a new home

From new beginnings to a new home, we celebrate a year in the life of Southbank Sinfonia. Each year, 33 of the most promising young musicians from around the world come together to form Southbank Sinfonia’s ‘orchestra like no other’. Given the challenges presented by the ongoing global health pandemic, the 2020/21 Fellowship certainly had ‘a year like no other’ and as their Principal Partner, we wanted to shine a spotlight on, and celebrate, their amazing achievements.
