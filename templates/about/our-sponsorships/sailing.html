The Ocean Race 2023

Team Malizia are competing in The Ocean Race, one of the toughest team challenges in sport. The Ocean Race is a five month, 32,000 nautical mile circumnavigation of the globe with eight global legs. The race started in Alicante, Spain on Sunday, 15 January 2023 and finishes in Genova, Italy towards the end of June 2023.

A Race We Must Win

Team Malizia is supporting the global effort to fight climate change. "A Race We Must Win" carries a double meaning: a sailing team competing to win each race and on a bigger scale, we're all united to win this race against climate change and global warming.
 

“We are delighted to announce our continued support for Team Malizia and Boris Herrmann, who have already accomplished so much. To compete at the highest level in sport takes strength of character, leadership, and teamwork, all attributes we aspire to share. With their scientific mission to find solutions to ocean and climate change, they will continue to raise awareness for sustainability issues around the globe. We are hugely excited by the prospect of what we can achieve together”

Giorgio Pradelli, CEO of EFG International

© Martin Keruzoré/Team Malizia

“I am proud and honoured to continue this successful and long partnership with EFG who were one of the first companies to join Team Malizia in 2016. Now we have an exciting 5 years planned together taking in some of the most iconic round the world races, The Ocean Race 2022 and The Vendée Globe 2024"

Boris Herrmann, Skipper of Team Malizia
