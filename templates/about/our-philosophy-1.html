Our philosophy

Bringing entrepreneurial thinking to Swiss private banking

A Swiss private banking experience built around you

We are passionate about delivering expert advice to our clients, managing their assets, financial and family affairs, and creating solutions that enable them to reach their financial ambitions in highly competitive markets. We want to earn your trust day by day. This is our inspiration.

We are a leading Swiss private banking group with global reach, shaping one of the most attractive growth markets in financial services. At EFG International, we have created a new banking experience and an entrepreneurial business model centred around our clients and the independent and customised advice our Client Relationship Officers (CRO) offer them.

We are convinced that the foundations for the successful management of a client relationship in wealth management are the sophistication and competitiveness of a leading global financial institution combined with the expertise, experience and empathy of our CROs. They have the tools and the experience needed to provide quality advice to each individual client and create customised solutions.

Technology is an important enabler for us but we also believe that the human touch and talent make all the difference.

Meeting the expectations of our clients is the measure of our success. We advise them in the spirit of entrepreneurship that has shaped our culture. Our ability to deliver the best solution and the best quality is rooted in our strong capital position, our sound risk management and our robust compliance framework.

The EFG Art Collection

We have always found affinity with those with passion for their craft and a unique view of the world. Our Corporate Art Collection contains over 1,500 works by artists from different nations and periods, tracing an arc through history from the Old Masters to the Avant-Garde to todays entrepreneurial thinkers.

Among the works are creations by some of Switzerland’s and Italy’s most inspiring artists, including Bernardino Luini, Franz Gertsch and Mario Merz, to name a few examples. We are proud to display these works in our headquarters as a reminder of the brilliance that can come from thinking differently.
